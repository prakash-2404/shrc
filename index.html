Our Journey

Since our inception in 2009, Sivasakthi Hotel & Resort Consultancy (SHRC) has been a trailblazer in the hospitality industry, transforming hotel businesses with innovative strategies and expert consultancy. With a passion for the art of hospitality, we have collaborated with numerous establishments, helping them achieve operational excellence, brand recognition, and sustainable growth.

One of our recent milestones is our strategic partnership with Srinith International, a hospitality venture in Tamil Nadu. Through this collaboration, we are committed to providing expert consulting services in venture creation, hotel operations, faculty training, business promotions, digital marketing, and revenue management. Our approach is rooted in a deep understanding of industry trends, customer behavior, and operational efficiency, ensuring our partners succeed in a highly competitive market.

At SHRC, we believe in continuous innovation and personalized solutions, ensuring each project we undertake is a success story. From business ideation and strategic planning to digital branding and revenue optimization, we empower hotels and resorts to thrive in the evolving hospitality landscape.
